Latitude vs. Humidity

What is the relationship between latitude and humidity?

Latitude and humidity show a relationship, but it's quite different than our Max Temp analyis, which showed a linear trend from higher temps at lower latitudes to lower temps at higher lats.

Here we see higher humidity readings at the lowest AND the highest latitudes, with lower levels of humidity at the in the mid-latitudes. The linear regression line doesn't really fit the data well, which shows a more complex relationship than a simply linear one.

We can see the higher humidities clearer when we look at the means by absolute value of latitude.
